Brief

To deliver a joined up marketing and social media strategy for The Waterways. Shoot a number of still photography sets aimed at the social media instagram/facebook/social audience, suited to the mobile first world.

Objectives

  • Work with the team to produce a social media campaign calendar to ensure the right photography, captions and hashtags are delivered at key times in order for them to have the most impact. This is key to what we do.
  • To use social media photography and captions to raise awareness of the location, food, drink (cocktails/mixology) and other aspects of The waterways W9.
  • To work with the team to stream line the marketing social media process. uploading/managing/signing off content, so that as little time/effort is spent
  • To gain likes and push the Waterways W9 up in key areas (#maidavale on instagram) using intelligent hashtagging and reciprocal liking.
  • Build a social media presence to attract customers back Retention.
  • Use newly built social media presence to attract new customers Aqusition.

We Will

  • Produce an the plate traditional food photography set, filtered using instagram with optimised professional captions and hastags.
  • Produce a mixology/cocktail photography set, filtered using instagram with optimised professional captions and hastags.
  • Produce an in location in action (at the The Waterways W9) photography set, using staff creating cocktails/serving food. Filtered using instagram with optimised professional captions and hastags.
  • Intelligently hashtag images and help the Waterways W9 produce its own hastag set
  • Ensure all images captions adhere to a certain style so the message remains on brand, cohesive and strong.
  • Help/guide the team in consolidating and planning their social media strategy.
  • Identify and monitor competitors, ensuring our content, strategy and delivery IS BETTER.

Telling the story

  • Use all of the points in 'We Will' to continuously tell the story of The Waterway. Ideally posting to instagram three times per day, with reposts to other channels, ie facebook/twitter.
  • Use all of the points in 'We Will' to build social media/marketing momentum continuiosly evolving 'The Story'. Bring on board new content quickly and adapt, as new menus/specials/drinks etc occur.
  • Tell a better story than the competion about the venues location, staff, food, drinks and experience.

The Team

Iconic Crew is a London-based independent stills and moving image production company specialising in fashion and beauty advertising and editorial - a collective of highly experienced producers whose knowledge of the industry is second to none.

The team is highly experienced in large scale marketing print/digital/online in a multi channel world. Specialising in social media and highly performant digital campaigns, our images and skills have been used to promote london nightclubs and a luxury watch brand.

Our personal instagram account theupshotpictures has a following of nearly 4000 people with posts attracting up to 550 likes, it also has a very large local and worldwide following.
